About Us

Saleema Foundation Mansfield, Texas, USA.

Our Vision

Saleema Foundation is an American nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of needy children and families and fostering tolerance and understanding between people of different faiths.

Our Mission

Empower needy children, women and the elderly with first aid, education and entrepreneurship gifts to help them better their lives and develop a better relationship with the Creator, with one's self and other fellow human beings.

Our Services

Saleema Foundation is a U.S.-based nonprofit organization committed to enhancing the quality of life for vulnerable children and families while promoting interfaith dialogue and understanding.
